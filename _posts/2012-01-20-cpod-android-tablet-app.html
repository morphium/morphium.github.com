--- layout: blog title: CPod Android Tablet/GoogleTV App Released ---

The fruit of my labor has finally been released and is available on the Android Market here.

The ChinesePod application for Android tablets and Google TVs provides ChinesePod subscribers with access to all of ChinesePod's award-winning lesson content (more than 2000 and growing) and study tools on both tablets and TVs. All of the lesson and review content that you'd expect on the ChinesePod.com website is available to you via this application.
